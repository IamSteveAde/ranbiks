Eland FC hold first aid training for Technical Crew.

In efforts to better prepare them for possible injuries and emergency situations that may arise in the course of carrying out their duties, a first aid clinic was held for members of the technical crew. The training lasted for a day with emphasises on familiarity with sports injuries that may occur during participation in football and the best first aid techniques to apply. Amongst other things, there was emphasis on carrying out Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) in case of an emergency and also how to place people in recovery positions for cases related to breathing and respiratory complications.

The training, conducted over the course of a day, delved deep into various aspects of first aid, with a particular focus on sports-related injuries commonly encountered in the realm of football. From sprains and strains to more serious scenarios like cardiac events, the crew was provided with the necessary knowledge and techniques to respond promptly and effectively to any situation that may arise.

One of the key highlights of the training was the emphasis on Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR), a life-saving technique crucial in instances of sudden cardiac arrest. Through hands-on demonstrations and simulations, participants gained confidence in performing CPR, thereby increasing the likelihood of saving lives in critical moments.

Additionally, the training addressed the importance of proper positioning and management of individuals experiencing breathing or respiratory complications. Crew members were educated on the correct procedures for placing individuals in recovery positions, a crucial skill that can significantly aid in maintaining airway patency and ensuring optimal oxygenation.

Beyond the technical aspects, the training fostered a culture of preparedness and teamwork among the crew members. By instilling a sense of responsibility and empowerment, Eland FC is not only prioritizing the safety of its personnel but also reinforcing the values of solidarity and camaraderie within the club.

Looking ahead, the implementation of such initiatives underscores Eland FC's commitment to excellence not only on the field but also in matters concerning the welfare of its members. As the club continues to strive for success, it does so with the assurance that its technical crew stands ready to face any challenge with competence and confidence, armed with the invaluable knowledge imparted through this first aid training program.
