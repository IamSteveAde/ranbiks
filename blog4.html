Eland FC appoints Paul Kafiya Club Secretary

In efforts to further enhance club professionalism and to improve club activities, Eland FC is proud to announce the appointment of Paul Kafiya as its Club Secretary. With a track record of diligence, dedication, professionalism and integrity, Paul stands as a great addition to what is already a strong team behind Eland FC. Everyone at Eland FC welcomes Paul to the team and looks forward to a fruitful and successful working relationship.

Paul's appointment heralds a new era of growth and innovation for Eland FC, as his proven leadership and organizational skills are poised to streamline club operations and enhance overall effectiveness. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for excellence, Paul is poised to play a pivotal role in shaping the future trajectory of the club.

Beyond his impressive credentials, Paul's appointment is a testament to Eland FC's unwavering commitment to fostering a culture of professionalism and accountability at every level. By entrusting key roles to individuals of Paul's caliber, the club reaffirms its dedication to excellence and its relentless pursuit of success both on and off the field.

As Paul assumes his new role, he joins an already formidable team behind Eland FC, united by a shared vision of excellence and a commitment to achieving greatness. The entire Eland FC community extends a warm welcome to Paul, eagerly anticipating the positive impact he will undoubtedly bring to the club.

With Paul Kafiya at the helm as Club Secretary, Eland FC embarks on a journey of continued growth, innovation, and success. Together, we look forward to forging a prosperous and fruitful working relationship, as we strive to reach new heights of achievement and excellence in the world of football.
